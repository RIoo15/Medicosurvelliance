Home Medicine Reviews

Ensuring That Patients Safely And Correctly Take The Proper Medications

By verifying the safety of multiple prescriptions from different GPs,
validating that the correct medications are being taken for certain
conditions, and ensuring the proper usage and timing of medicines,
we promote patient health and wellness while preventing
medicine-related hospitalisations.

What is HMR?

A Home Medicines Review is when an accredited consultant
pharmacist reviews the medicines that you are taking while
ensuring that you’re taking them correctly.


By offering at-home or in-residence visits, the pharmacist
provides a comprehensive assessment that includes guidance
for usage, timelines, side-effects, prescription medications, and
patient questions.

How do I arrange for a HMR?

It's easy.
First, speak with your doctor to ask for a referral.

From there, the doctor will securely provide Medicosurveillance
with information about your medical history.

Lastly, we will reach out to you and set up a time to meet in the comfort of your home.

Why would I want a Home Medicines Review?

A Home Medications Review can be a key to helping you control your health care if you suffer
from chronic conditions like diabetes, cardiovascular disease, asthma or any other health
conditions.

Working together with your GP and pharmacist, you
can guarantee that your medicines:

Meet your healthcare and treatment outcome
Can be taken safely together
Are taken correctly as prescribed

At the same time, you will:

Increase your knowledge
Build confidence
Embrace independence
Improve your quality of life
