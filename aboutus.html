About Medicosurveillance

Who We Are and What We Do

Who We Are and What We Do?

As accredited consultant pharmacists, we proudly provide HMR,
RMMR, and QUM services to ensure the health and wellness of
patients.

Our services take a detail-oriented approach to medicine review,
offering custom-tailored suggestions that are based on each
unique patient. Whether you’re at home or in a residential care
facility, our dedicated team will travel to your location to provide
care.

Our mission is to ensure peace of mind for general
practitioners, patients, and their families. With a focus on
quality and compassionate care, we ensure that patients
have the right medicine and clear guidance for better health
and wellness.

Practice Partners

At Medicosurveillance, we understand that healthcare is about
people.

We care about our patients and sharing responsibility for
healthcare with our network of doctors and pharmacies by
optimising patient’s medication management is an important step
in improving their quality of life.

By working in partnership with doctors, pharmacies and other allied
health professionals, we have been able to deliver a positive
healthcare outcome for our patients that aligns with their values,
beliefs and lifestyle.

We strongly believe that every patient must be an active
participant in decisions that support their healthcare plans and
remains sacrosanct to us here at Medicosurveillance.

Other Services

Dependable Residential Medication Management Reviews

Helping Residential Aged Care Patients Navigate Their
Medication Regimen

Whether a patient is unable to take their medicine as prescribed
or likely to make mistakes after a recent prescription change, our
RMMRs improve quality of life, prevent dangerous discrepancies or
misuse, and provide greater consistency and control for the best
possible outcome.

Comprehensive Quality Use of Medicines (QUM) Services

Facilitating Suitable Medicine Decision-Making for Patients
While Minimising Usage Risks

By carefully considering each patient’s unique circumstances, we
ensure the appropriateness of medicine recommendations while
gauging each patient's ability to correctly take medications. As a
result, we seek to reduce the annual 230,000 hospital admissions
caused by adverse medicine events.

Other Services

At Medicosurveillance, we understand that our patients and their loved
ones have questions about our medicine review services. Here are the
most common questions we receive:

Q. Am I eligible for a Home Medicine Review?

To get started with your HMR, first, verify your eligibility by visiting our eligible patient page.

Q. How do I start the process for a HMR or RMMR?

Speak to your doctor to discuss if a HMR or RMMR is right for you. Visit our HMR Page for more information

Q. Am I eligible for a Home Medicine Review?

We provide our services in the comfort of your home or residential care facility.

Q. Am I eligible for a Home Medicine Review?

Yes. We take an in-depth look at your prescription and non-prescription medications to ensure that there are
no dangerous combinations.

Q. Am I eligible for a Home Medicine Review?

Unless there was a significant medical status or prescription change within the past 12 months, you are not
eligible for a second HMR.
