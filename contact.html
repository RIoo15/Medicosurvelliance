How Can We Help?

Get in Touch with Medicosurveillance

If you or your patients need comprehensive Home Medicine
Review (HMR), Residential Medication Management Review
(RMMR), or Quality Use of Medicines (QUM) Services, we are
here to help.

Get in touch today and we will discuss how our accredited
pharmacists at Medicosurveillance can help ensure your
safety and comfort. Fill out the contact form on the right to
learn more.


HMR Referrals

For general practitioners, we can be reached using the
contact form on the right. To start the process, please
ensure that you have your patient’s medical history ready in
your referral letter.

Upon completion of the HMR, we will schedule a time to
meet with you to discuss the results.
